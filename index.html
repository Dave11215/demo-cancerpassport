What you might gain

  • Early access to promising treatments (when eligible).
  • Expert care and more frequent monitoring by specialists.
  • A voice in research that helps future patients.

Your protections

  • You decide Join or leave anytime—your regular care won’t be affected.
  • IRB oversight Independent boards review study safety and ethics.
  • Privacy Your health information is protected under HIPAA.

Common concern

Myth “I might get only a placebo.”

Reality: Cancer trials rarely use a placebo alone. You’ll typically receive at least the standard-of-care treatment, with the new therapy added when randomized.

Impact

96% of cancer patients in trials would recommend participation to others.

Source: peer-reviewed syntheses of patient-reported trial experiences.
